VerseWorks Changelog
Version 1.2.2
====

Release status: Alpha / Experimental
Minecraft: 1.21.1
NeoForge: 21.1.219

Highlights
 - Create unstable custom worlds with Hyperbooks and Verses.
 - Explore generated VerseWorks dimensions with custom environmental hazards.
 - Discover mystic crystals, warp growth, and ancient mystic ruins.
 - Use operator-only VerseWorks debug commands for testing and world inspection.

Notes
 - This release includes the third-party VerseWorks entry safeguard and ships as VerseWorks 1.2.2.
 - This is still an early experimental release and world behavior may continue to change.
 - Existing alpha worlds may not remain compatible with future updates.
